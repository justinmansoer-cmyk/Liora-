# Liora North Star

Liora is een cozy life simulation game about community, discovery and meaningful relationships.

The goal is not farming, combat or grinding.

The goal is to create a town that feels alive, where every resident matters and where players continue playing because they genuinely care about the world and its people.

Core pillars:

- Living NPCs
- Meaningful relationships
- Curiosity and discovery
- Warmth and comfort
- A believable town
- Small moments that matter

Every future decision must support these pillars.
