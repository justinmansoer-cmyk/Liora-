# Liora Game Bible

## Genre

Cozy life simulation.

## Core Experience

The player lives in a small town where people matter.

The focus is on relationships, discovery, community and everyday life.

## Not About

- Combat
- Grinding
- Endless farming
- Saving the world
- Power fantasy

## Pillars

### Living NPCs

Every resident should feel like a real person.

### Meaningful Relationships

Friendships should develop naturally over time.

### Discovery

Players should regularly discover something new.

### Community

The town should feel connected and alive.

### Comfort

The game should feel relaxing without becoming boring.

## Art Direction

Warm.
Inviting.
Readable.
Timeless.

## World Design

A compact town is preferred over a huge empty world.

Quality over size.

## Design Rule

Whenever a new feature is proposed:

Ask:

"Does this strengthen relationships, discovery, community or comfort?"

If not, it probably does not belong in Liora.
